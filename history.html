The Evolution of the Presidency

The office of the President has grown from a small role into one of the most powerful positions in the world.

1789: The Beginning

George Washington set the standard, proving that a leader could lead with honor and then step down voluntarily.

1861: The Great Test

During the Civil War, the presidency became a symbol of national unity under Abraham Lincoln.

Today: The 21st Century

Modern presidents now manage a global economy and use social media to speak directly to the people.
